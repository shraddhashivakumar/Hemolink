Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about donating and requesting blood

Who can donate blood? +
Any healthy individual aged between 18 and 60 years and weighing more than 50kg can donate blood.
How does Hemolink find nearby donors? +
Hemolink uses location-based matching to identify donors within a 1 km radius and sends instant alerts via SMS and email.
Is donating blood safe? +
Yes. Sterile and disposable equipment is used every time, ensuring no risk of infection.
How often can I donate? +
Whole blood can be donated every 3 months for men and every 4 months for women as per medical guidelines.
What happens after I accept a request? +
The seeker receives your confirmation and the location details so you can coordinate the donation quickly.
